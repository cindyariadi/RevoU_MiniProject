BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight, determining the ideal weight category and preparing for a weight loss program.

This calculator should only be used by adults, pregnant women cannot rely on these BMI readings.

The calculator will give you an idea of ​​how your weight compares to common values. BMI is calculated as your weight (KG) divided by the square of your height or = Kg/M2
